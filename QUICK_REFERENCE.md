## 📈 Nutritional Focus

### Key Minerals for CKD:

| Mineral | Goal | Why |
|---------|------|-----|
| **K** (Potassium) | <300mg/serving | Heart & nerve function |
| **P** (Phosphorus) | <150mg/serving | Bone & heart health |
| **Na** (Sodium) | <85mg/serving | Blood pressure control |
| **Protein** | 20-35g/serving | Body function (limited) |
| **Fiber** | 1-3g/serving | Digestive health |
