About Renal-Friendly Cooking

Our renal-friendly recipes are specially developed to help manage Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) by limiting:

  • Sodium: Reduced salt intake to help control blood pressure
  • Potassium: Limited potassium to protect kidney function
  • Phosphorus: Reduced phosphorus to prevent bone problems
  • Protein: Appropriate protein portions for kidney health
